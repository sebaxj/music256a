Sequencer Ideas:




It appears that at the most basic definition, a music sequencer is a set of software or hardware which can record and/or playback a series of notes programmed by a musician. One of the first sequencers in "Western Music" was the piano roll. I found the piano roll a simple example to learn about what should/shouldn't be the capability of a music sequencer. Furthermore, it appears that the evolution of music sequencers has gradually favored software over hardware, eventually bringing us MIDI. As such, I want to use the music sequencer project to explore how the literal, physiological aspects of human are responsible for music just as is the higher level cortex we have developed over millions of years of evolution.
