Learning Chunity

So far, I have thoroughly enjoyed learning ChucK/Unity/Chunity. I found the tutorials pretty straight forward and I love having the newfound power/ability to create multi-dimensional interactive projects where sound and visuals work in tandem. I do enjoy the scripting more than the 3D modeling, mainly because I find it harder to strike a balance between functionality and aesthetics where as code is more straight forward. I am still looking into how to optimize/fix this.

Ideas for my Sound Visualizer

The Sound Visualizer project presents me with the unique opportunity to explore my primary interest at school, Pre-med, in the domain of music, my major. As such, my ideas reflect my desire to visualize how music affects our bodies.

  • Brain: my first idea involves using the visualizer to literally explore artistically the impact of music on the brain. I want to not only visualize the time and frequency domain of music, but also the domain emotion in the music and reflect that into a model the brain.
  • Basilar Membrane: my second idea is to present the frequency domain as modeled cochlear basilar membrane to illustrate how our ears 'FFT-ize' sound. For the time domain I want to incorporate a beating heart and display the waveform as electrical impulses.
